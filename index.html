About

Mammal Animal Detector (Sulawesi) is a final project designed to provide information about mammals that breed, especially in the province of Sulawesi. The purpose of making this project is to achieve final results and assets as well as increase productivity or motivation, time bound, and predictable specifics at the end of the project.
